Sample Apps

Hello World

Not sure where to start? Take a look at our at_hello_world app to see how some of the common verbs and methods of the @protocol are applied and implemented!

at_cookbook

This is an intermediate-level @platform based application that uses the verbs we learned in the at_hello_world application to make a working cookbook for the chef inside of us all!

@mosphere

@mosphere is a peer to peer secure file transfer app - this works across all mobile devices & with/out Wi-Fi.

at_chats

This directory contains the complete and incomplete code for the @platform messaging application. Feel free to use this for testing, debugging, or just trying out the app!
